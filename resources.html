Resources
Have questions about God, Jesus, the Bible, or theology? Whether you’re searching for answers, exploring faith, or looking to grow deeper in God’s Word, here are some trusted resources to help you on the journey:
🌐 Online Resources
  • Got Questions – Straightforward, Bible-based answers to thousands of spiritual questions.
  • Bible Society – Access “You've Got The Time”, the New Testament read aloud in a fresh, modern
  • Bible Gateway – Read and search the Bible in many translations, with devotionals, commentaries, and study plans.
  • Bible Study Tools – A full library of concordances, dictionaries, and study helps to deepen your understanding of Scripture.
📱 Mobile Apps
Carry the Bible with you wherever you go:
  • YouVersion Bible App – Free, with 1000+ translations, audio Bibles, devotionals, and reading plans.
  • Olive Tree Bible App – Powerful study tools and resources available even offline.
📚 Local & Printed Resources
If you enjoy the richness of printed books:
GOOD LIVING BOOKSHOP – Conveniently located on Mill Street (Bideford), just in front of our church. In our church’s Welcome Area, you’ll also find a small library of donated books, leaflets, and a selection of CDs/DVDs. Please feel free to take and use any of these resources.
